Thanks to our gift donors!

Your gifts make it possible to bring happiness to the children.
500,000+ kids spend time in foster care each year.
For many children, Christmas time can be sad and lonely because they are undergoing stressful situations due to poverty, illness, or lack of resources. With your generosity and kindness, we are able to provide a moment of happiness to children who are in need. By creating this platform, I wish to provide effortless and convenient ways to share the joy in the holiday season.
