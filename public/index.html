Our Super Easy & Transparent Donation Process

Step 1. Browse wish cards & Find one you like

Step 2. Click 'Donate Gift' button on the wish card

Step 3. Once it automatically opens an Amazon page, Click 'Continue' to add the child's wish item to your Amazon cart

Step 4. Proceed to checkout on Amazon

Step 5. ***IMPORTANT*** Under 'Other Addresses", Make sure to select the registered address of the foster child

Step 6. ***IMPORTANT*** Before you check out, double check that shipping address is set to the gift recipient's address. Place your order. Now, your gift is on its way!
